Welcome to Finance Tracker!

You're all set up. This quick guide will show you everything you need to know to take control of your money — it only takes a minute.

Track your income, spending, and savings all in one place
Your data syncs to your account — access it from anywhere
Everything is private and secured with your password
🏷️

Start with Categories

Categories help you organise where your money goes. Create ones that match your life — Food, Transport, Entertainment, whatever makes sense for you.

Hit + Category on the dashboard to create your first one
Every expense you log gets tagged to a category
The Spending Report groups your transactions by category automatically
💸

Log Income & Spending

Use the Quick Actions buttons on the dashboard to record money coming in or going out. Every transaction is saved instantly to your account.

+ Income adds funds to your total balance
− Spend logs an expense and deducts it from your balance
± Savings moves money between your balance and a separate savings pot
🎯

Set Savings Goals

Got something you're saving towards? Create a goal with a target amount and contribute to it from your savings. Watch the progress bar fill up as you get closer.

Find Savings Goals in the sidebar to create your first goal
Contributions come from your savings pot, not your main balance
When you hit 100% you'll get a little celebration 🎉
🔁

Recurring Transactions

Got fixed monthly expenses like rent, subscriptions, or a gym membership? Mark them as recurring and they'll be auto-logged every month on the date you set.

When logging a spend, tick Repeat monthly and enter the day
Each time you open the app, due transactions are auto-logged instantly
Manage or remove recurring entries from the Monthly Recurring card on the dashboard
✨

You're all set!

That's everything. Head to your dashboard and start tracking. Here's a suggested order to get going:

1. Create a few categories that match your spending
2. Add your current income or starting balance
3. Set a savings goal to work towards
4. Add any recurring monthly expenses so they auto-log!
